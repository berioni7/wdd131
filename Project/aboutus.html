Who we are?

The Big Cinema was born from a true passion

Founded in 2025 by Gabriel Berioni, a student at BYU-Idaho, this site is more than just a space to talk about movies — it’s a tribute to the power of cinema to move, transform, and connect people. From a very young age, Gabriel was captivated by the stories told on screen, the magic of screenwriting, the beauty of cinematography, and the intensity of acting. The Big Cinema is, therefore, the reflection of a lifelong love for the seventh art.

Our goal is to celebrate cinema in all its forms

Here, you’ll find content dedicated to writers, directors, actors, composers, cinematographers, and editors — the names that bring films to life, both in front of and behind the camera. Our mission is to honor the talent and dedication of all professionals who build the worlds we see on the big screen.

Respect is the foundation of everything we do

At The Big Cinema, we believe that art should be celebrated in a welcoming and respectful environment. We’ve created this space for movie lovers, enthusiasts, and experts to explore content freely and meaningfully, always with empathy and inclusion.

Content that informs and inspires

Our site features in-depth articles, curiosities, themed lists, artist profiles, and analyses that dive deep into the art of filmmaking. We aim to deliver relevant information with accessible language and a pleasant visual design, so every visitor feels part of this community driven by a love for powerful storytelling.

A journey that’s just getting started

The Big Cinema is a project that continues to grow. With dedication, care, and plenty of research, we strive to offer an ever-richer experience for our readers. We value every piece of feedback, every visit, and every person who takes the time to explore what we’ve built with so much heart.

The Big Cinema team thanks you for your visit!
